Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation. It is the sequel to the original Dragon Ball series and follows the adventures of Goku as he defends the Earth against powerful foes.

The series is known for its intense battles, character development, and iconic transformations such as Super Saiyan. It has become a cultural phenomenon and has spawned numerous movies, spin-offs, and merchandise.
